Gratisproben vs Coupon Sites: Introduction

When it comes to getting free products and maximum savings in Germany, two popular approaches stand out: Gratisproben (free samples) and coupon sites. Gratisproben involve requesting free trial-size products directly from manufacturers or through aggregation platforms, while coupon sites provide discounts and promotional codes that reduce the price of purchases you were already planning to make. Both methods have passionate followings, and both deliver real value to savvy consumers.

The German market is particularly rich in both types of opportunities. Germany's strong consumer protection laws, robust postal system, and competitive retail landscape create an ideal environment for companies to distribute free samples and for retailers to offer aggressive coupon promotions. Platforms like TryTreat combine both approaches, allowing users to access Gratisproben and coupons from a single convenient dashboard.

In this comparison, we examine which approach delivers more free stuff, which requires less effort, and how combining both strategies can maximize the total value you receive each month. Whether you are a dedicated freebie hunter or a casual saver looking to reduce your monthly expenses, this guide will help you optimize your approach.

What Are Gratisproben?

Gratisproben are free sample products that companies distribute to consumers as a marketing strategy. The concept is simple: companies provide small trial-size versions of their products at no cost, hoping that consumers who try the product will become paying customers. In Germany, Gratisproben are available through a wide variety of channels including manufacturer websites, social media promotions, in-store sampling events, and dedicated free sample platforms.

The range of products available as Gratisproben in Germany is remarkably diverse. On any given day, you might find free samples of food and beverages, cosmetics and skincare products, cleaning supplies, pet food, supplements, and even household gadgets. The samples are typically sent by mail to your home address and arrive within 1-3 weeks of requesting them.

The primary appeal of Gratisproben is that they are completely free. There is no purchase requirement, no subscription fee, and no obligation to buy anything. You simply fill out a request form with your name and address, submit it, and wait for the sample to arrive. This zero-cost, zero-commitment model makes Gratisproben accessible to virtually everyone, regardless of budget.

Popular German Gratisproben platforms aggregate offers from hundreds of brands, making it easy to find new samples daily. TryTreat's free samples section is regularly updated with new offers, ensuring you never run out of products to try.

What Are Coupon Sites?

Coupon sites aggregate promotional codes, printable vouchers, and digital offers from retailers and brands across Germany. Unlike Gratisproben, which provide free products, coupon sites offer discounts on purchases you make at regular or reduced prices. The goal is to help you spend less money on products you were already planning to buy.

Coupon sites typically organize their offers by retailer, product category, or discount type. Popular categories on German coupon sites include grocery coupons, fashion discount codes, electronics deals, and food delivery promotions. Many coupon sites also feature exclusive codes negotiated directly with retailers that cannot be found elsewhere.

The savings from coupon sites can be substantial, particularly for planned purchases. A well-timed coupon code can save you 20-50% on fashion items, €10-30 on grocery orders, or 15-30% on electronics. For online shopping, coupon codes are entered at checkout to instantly reduce your total, while physical coupons can be presented at brick-and-mortar stores.

However, coupon sites have an inherent limitation: they only save you money on purchases you make. If you are looking for truly free products with no spending required, coupons will not deliver that. You still need to pay for the item, just at a reduced price.

Head-to-Head: Gratisproben vs Coupon Sites

When comparing Gratisproben and coupon sites directly, several key dimensions emerge:

Total cost to you: Gratisproben are completely free, requiring zero spending. Coupon sites reduce your spending but still require a purchase. For pure cost savings, Gratisproben win decisively.

Product size and value: Gratisproben provide trial-size products worth €0.50-5.00 each. Coupons provide discounts on full-size products worth €5-100+. For receiving full-size products, coupon sites are better, but you still need to pay something.

Volume of opportunities: On any given day, there are typically more active Gratisproben offers than coupon offers for any specific retailer. However, coupon sites offer more opportunities across the entire retail landscape, including services and subscriptions where free samples are not possible.

Effort required: Both require similar effort for finding and requesting offers. Gratisproben require a request form submission, while coupons require finding and applying codes. The effort difference is minimal.

Variety of products: Gratisproben offer a narrower range of product types (mainly consumer goods), while coupons cover virtually every product category including electronics, travel, and services.

Long-term value: Building a habit of using both Gratisproben and coupons creates a comprehensive savings strategy that covers both free products and discounted purchases. Users who leverage both approaches through platforms like TryTreat report the highest overall savings.

Combining Gratisproben and Coupons for Maximum Value

The most effective approach is not choosing between Gratisproben and coupon sites but combining both into a unified savings strategy. Here is how to maximize value from both sources:

Step 1: Use Gratisproben to discover new products at zero cost. Try samples of products you are curious about before committing to full-size purchases. This prevents wasted spending on products you might not like.

Step 2: When you find a product you love through a free sample, search for coupons to purchase the full-size version at a discount. Many brands that offer free samples also have active coupon promotions running simultaneously.

Step 3: Route your purchases through cashback platforms for additional savings on top of coupon discounts.

Step 4: Share your experiences on social media or through review platforms. Many brands reward reviewers with additional free products or exclusive coupon codes.

This cyclical approach of trying, buying, and reviewing creates a virtuous cycle where free samples inform smart purchases, coupons reduce purchase costs, and reviews generate future free product opportunities. Users who master this cycle through TryTreat report receiving €200-400 worth of free or heavily discounted products every month.

If your primary goal is receiving free products with zero spending, Gratisproben are the clear winner. They provide trial-size products at no cost and require minimal effort. If your goal is saving money on planned purchases, coupon sites deliver more overall value through significant discounts on full-size products.
