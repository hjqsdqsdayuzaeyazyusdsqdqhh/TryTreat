Frequently Asked Questions

Loyalty points can typically be redeemed for discounts on purchases, free products, gift cards, travel vouchers, and charitable donations. The best option depends on your rewards program.

Redeem during bonus point multiplier events or when special redemption offers are available. Some programs offer better point-to-value ratios during promotional periods.

Many programs have expiration dates for unused points. Check your program's terms and set reminders to redeem before your points expire.

Some programs like payback allow point accumulation across multiple retailers. For other programs, you typically cannot combine points between different loyalty schemes.
