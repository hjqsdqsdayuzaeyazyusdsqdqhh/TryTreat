What Is TryTreat?

TryTreat is a comprehensive platform designed to help consumers in Germany find the best free samples, deals, coupons, cashback offers, and giveaways all in one place. Our mission is to simplify the process of saving money and discovering new products by curating offers from thousands of brands and retailers.

Unlike individual brand websites or scattered deal forums, TryTreat consolidates offers across multiple categories including free samples, coupons, cashback offers, giveaways, product testing, and loyalty programs. This centralized approach saves you time and ensures you never miss a valuable offer.

TryTreat also provides educational content including guides, FAQs, and tips to help you make the most of every savings opportunity. Whether you are a beginner just starting to explore freebies or a seasoned deal hunter looking for advanced strategies, TryTreat has resources tailored to your level.

Our platform is built on principles of transparency, trust, and consumer empowerment. Every offer we list is verified for legitimacy, and we clearly disclose any terms or conditions associated with each deal.

TryTreat's commitment to quality means that every listing on our platform undergoes a verification process before being published. This includes checking the legitimacy of the offer, confirming the brand's identity, reviewing terms and conditions, and ensuring the offer is currently active. This vetting process saves you time and reduces the risk of encountering fraudulent offers.

How to Navigate TryTreat

Getting started with TryTreat is simple. The platform is organized into clear categories that make it easy to find the type of offers you are looking for. The main navigation includes sections for free samples, coupons, cashback, giveaways, product testing, and rewards programs.

The search function allows you to look for specific brands, products, or types of offers. If you are looking for beauty samples, for example, you can search for "beauty" or "cosmetics" to see all relevant offers across categories.

Each offer page provides comprehensive details including a description of the offer, any terms and conditions, the expiration date, and a direct link to claim the offer. Our goal is to give you all the information you need to make an informed decision without having to hunt for details.

Guides and FAQ pages like this one provide in-depth information on specific topics. Our guides cover everything from general free sample information to coupon code strategies and cashback optimization.

The filter and sorting options on TryTreat help you narrow down offers based on your preferences. Filter by category, brand, or offer type to quickly find what you are looking for. Sorting options let you view the newest offers first, the most popular deals, or offers expiring soon.

Understanding TryTreat's Categories

TryTreat organizes offers into six main categories, each serving a different savings strategy:

  • Free Samples - Complimentary products from brands, also known as Gratisproben. Request products to try before you buy at no cost
  • Coupons - Discount codes and promotional offers that reduce your purchase price at checkout
  • Cashback - Money-back offers that return a percentage of your spending after purchase
  • Giveaways - Competitions and sweepstakes where you can win prizes ranging from small items to high-value rewards
  • Product Testing - Opportunities to test full-size products and provide feedback in exchange for keeping the product
  • Rewards Programs - Loyalty programs and points-based systems that reward your ongoing patronage

Each category offers unique benefits, and the best savings strategies typically involve engaging with multiple categories simultaneously. For detailed information on any category, explore the respective FAQ and guide pages on TryTreat.

We are continuously expanding our categories and features based on user feedback. If there is a type of deal or savings opportunity you would like to see on TryTreat, let us know through our feedback form. Our platform evolves based on the needs of our community.
