Frequently Asked Questions

Legitimate offers come from known brands or verified platforms. They never ask for payment information, bank details, or excessive personal data. Check the company's website and reviews.

Never provide your bank account number, credit card details, Social Security number, or password. Legitimate samples only need your name and shipping address.

If you provide excessive personal information to a scam site, identity theft is possible. Only sign up for samples through verified, reputable sources.

Report scams to the Verbraucherzentrale (consumer protection office), your local police, and the platform where you found the offer. You can also report fraudulent websites to the Federal Network Agency.
